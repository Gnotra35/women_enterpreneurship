1. Celebrating Women Empowerment: Insights from the First State Workshop on Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) in Goa (AIRWE)

ਸੋ ਕਿਉ ਮੰਦਾ ਆਖੀਐ ਜਿਤੁ ਜੰਮਹਿ ਰਾਜਾਨ ॥

So why call her bad? From her, kings are born. (SGGS Ang 473)

It was a humbling experience at the First state workshop under the Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP), a transformative event organized by NITI Aayog, Govt. of India, and the Goa State Government. This inspiring gathering took place on October 3, 2023, at the scenic CSIR-National Institute of Oceanography (NIO), Dona Paula, Goa, and brought together remarkable minds dedicated to empowering women entrepreneurs. The significance of women's entrepreneurship, underscoring the crucial role of mentorship in this journey was explored. The passion and deep sense of purpose was emphasized and the fundamental need for education forms the edifice upon which women can build their entrepreneurial dreams. Akal Institute of Rural Empowerment, a commendable initiative, is taking giant strides in educating and empowering women across five states in rural North India. The event was graced by NITI Aayog Chairman Mr. B.V.R Subramanyam and the Hon’ble Chief Minister of Goa, Dr. Pramod Sawant, who wholeheartedly supported the cause of nurturing women in business.

- Dr. Davinder Singh, The Kalgidhar Society Akal Academy Baru Sahib

2. Leadership Lessons by Mandeep Kaur Tangra at Eternal University

Eternal University welcomes Mandeep Kaur Tangra CEO SimbaQuartz on 11th June, 2022.

Mandeep Kaur Tangra is a motivational speaker, trainer and social entrepreneur by profession. She is the founder of a series of ventures -SimbaQuartz, Simbacart, SimbaCourse, and SimbaHire. She believes in not only increasing profits for businesses, but also in giving back to society. In 2016, ASSOCHAM India honoured her as a woman entrepreneur. Her startup “Simbacart” has been awarded as "The Upcoming Startup of the year 2016" by ASSOCHAM India. She was also honoured with the 2019 "Alumni Award for Social Entrepreneurship." She has transformed her village Tangra to an "IT Village" in order to create jobs, reduce village-to-city migration, and brain drain. Her areas of practice include IT Services, Digital Marketing, E-commerce, Start-up ecosystem, Corporate social responsibility & Philanthropy. Her social campaigns “More Smiles Each Day” and "50000 Shoe To Give" are the most popular ones that grabbed the attention of the masses in the country and overseas.

3.IDEA-GRAM
"The wealth of ideas by the wealth of nation"

With our commitment to support women entrepreneurs, Eternal University has established the Center of Excellence (COE) in Entrepreneurship and Innovation, alongside accelerating the setup of an Atal Community Incubation Centre (ACIC) under the Atal Innovation Mission, Niti Ayog, Government of India. Facilitated by a Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV) and a 4,000 square feet allocation on campus, the inaugural workshop with the Women Entrepreneurs Network took place on May 13, 2024. Additionally, the WEP Nodal Centre for Himachal Pradesh is slated to be established at Eternal University, Baru Sahib. These efforts aim to nurture a conducive ecosystem within the Akal Network, uplifting 20,000 women socially and economically in Himachal Pradesh.
